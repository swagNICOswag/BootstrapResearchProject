PlayStation 2

The PlayStation 2, released in 2000, revolutionized the gaming landscape, becoming one of the best-selling consoles of all time. With its powerful hardware, the PS2 introduced players to a rich library of games that spanned diverse genres, from iconic titles like "Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas" to immersive RPGs like "Final Fantasy X." Its backward compatibility with PlayStation games and the ability to play DVDs made it a multifunctional entertainment hub for households. The console's enduring popularity was bolstered by innovative features like online gaming and a robust selection of peripherals, solidifying its legacy in gaming history and influencing future consoles.
